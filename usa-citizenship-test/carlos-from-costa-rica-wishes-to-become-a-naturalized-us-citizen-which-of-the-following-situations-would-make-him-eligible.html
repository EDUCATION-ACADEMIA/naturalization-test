Carlos from Costa Rica wishes to become a naturalized U.S. citizen. Which of the following situations would make him eligible?

(A) ** He is 20 years old, has lived in the United States for seven years, and is able to speak and read basic English.

(B) He is 20 years old, has lived in the United States for three years, and knows the basic history of the United States.

(C) He is 16 years old, has lived in the United States for six years, and knows the basic history of the United States

(D) He is 16 years old, has lived in the United States for five years, and is able to speak and read basic English.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Naturalization (or naturalisation) is the legal act or process by which a non-citizen of a country may acquire citizenship or nationality of that country.

Concept note-2: -Naturalization is the process by which U.S. Citizenship is granted to a foreign citizen or national after he or she fulfills the requirements established by Congress in the Immigration and Nationality Act (INA).
