USA CITIZENSHIP TEST

[SOURCES]
The first step in becoming a citizen is

(A) ** live 5 years in the USA

(B) Apply to be a citizen

(C) Have an interview and take a test

(D) Take an oath of allegiance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As a permanent resident, you are generally eligible for naturalization after five years. This is the most common way that people apply to become a U.S. citizen. To qualify, you must have lived in the U.S. continuously for the five years immediately preceding the date you file Form N-400, Application for Naturalization.

Concept note-2: -In general, you may qualify for naturalization if you are at least 18 years old and have been a permanent resident for at least 5 years (or 3 years if you are married to a U.S. citizen) and meet all other eligibility requirements.

Concept note-3: -On average, it takes 18 months to 24 months to complete the naturalization process and become a U.S. citizen. The naturalization process has five general steps. It begins with filing Form N-400 and ends with taking the Oath of Allegiance.
