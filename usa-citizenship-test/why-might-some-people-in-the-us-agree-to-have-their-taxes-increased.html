Why might some people in the U.S. agree to have their taxes increased?

(A) To build more churches

(B) To get people to vote in elections

(C) ** To pay for public schools

(D) To help businesses earn more money

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Taxes are levied by governments on their citizens to generate income for undertaking projects to boost the economy of the country and to raise the standard of living of its citizens.

Concept note-2: -The property tax has been the single largest source of local revenue for schools in the United States, reflecting a strong culture of local control and a preference for local provision. Property taxation and school funding are closely linked in the United States.

Concept note-3: -Social Responsibility and Contribution to the Country If all income earners will pay the right amount of tax, the government can collect more money to support its objectives such as building roads, schools, better government salaries and improve government services.

Concept note-4: -The higher earnings realized by more highly educated people result in higher tax payments and higher payments to social support and insurance programs, such as Social Security and Medicare.
