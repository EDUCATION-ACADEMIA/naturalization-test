What is the term for a type of government in which one person possesses unlimited power?

(A) Communism

(B) Democracy

(C) Socialism

(D) ** Autocracy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A government system in which unlimited power is in the hands of one person is referred to as an autocracy.

Concept note-2: -An autocracy is a government controlled by one person with absolute power. As unlimited power doesn’t usually bring out the best in people, autocracies are often brutal regimes. The word autocracy comes from the Greek roots auto ("self") and kratos ("power").

Concept note-3: -Autocracy is a system of government in which absolute power over a state is concentrated in the hands of one person, whose decisions are subject neither to external legal restraints nor to regularized mechanisms of popular control (except perhaps for the implicit threat of a coup d’état or other forms of rebellion).

Concept note-4: -: the authority or rule of an autocrat. : government in which one person possesses unlimited power.

Concept note-5: -Autocracy – System of Government A system of government in which the complete powers of a state or a nation are concentrated in the hands of one single person is known as an autocracy.
