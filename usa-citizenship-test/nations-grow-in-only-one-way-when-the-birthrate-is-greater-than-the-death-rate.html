Nations grow in only one way-when the birthrate is greater than the death rate

(A) ** false

(B) true

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Growth through natural increase occurs when the birth rate exceeds the death rate.

Concept note-2: -Population change is governed by the balance between birth rates and death rates. If the birth rate stays the same and the death rate decreases, then population numbers will grow. If the birth rate increases and the death rate stays the same, then population will also grow.

Concept note-3: -Now, when the death rate is equal to the birth rate the population growth is zero that means there is no increase and decrease of population in that particular year.

Concept note-4: -Birth rate, death rate and migration rate affect the population of a country. When the birth rate increases and the death rate decreases, the population of a country increases. When the birth rate decreases and the death rate increases, the population of a country decreases.
