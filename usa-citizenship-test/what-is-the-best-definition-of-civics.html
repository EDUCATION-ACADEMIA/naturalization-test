What is the best definition of Civics?

(A) ** the study of the rights and duties of citizenship.

(B) the study of the rights and duties of the clergy.

(C) the study of the rights and duties of media.

(D) the study of the rights and duties of warfare.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Civics is the analysis of the research and practice of citizenship, including its rights and responsibilities; citizens’ responsibilities to one another as part of a larger political body and to the administration.

Concept note-2: -: a social science dealing with the rights and duties of citizens.

Concept note-3: -The study of citizenship is called civics. If your school offers a civics class, you can learn about the importance of things like voting and paying taxes. Student government can teach you about civics, and so can certain social studies lessons and classes that a country’s new citizens sometimes take.

Concept note-4: -It’s more than a national curriculum subject Citizenship education involves developing the knowledge, skills and confidence to enable people to make their own decisions and to take responsibility for their own lives and communities.
