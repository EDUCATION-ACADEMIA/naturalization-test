How is contributing to food banks to feed the hungry a civic responsibility?

(A) Citizens are obeying the law

(B) Citizens are obeying the law

(C) Citizens are ensuring domestic tranquility.

(D) ** Citizens are contributing to the common good.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The idea is simple: Organizations which discard food in acceptable condition, offer such food to individuals in need or organizations which can use the food to help others. This food can be either donated or sold at considerable lower prices.

Concept note-2: -Donating to the needy is a great way to improve the conditions in your neighborhood or community. Donating food to the worthy people or organizations helps counter poverty, hunger and at the same time, it can improve harmony, friendliness, and trust among residents.
