The main purpose of a census is

(A) ** to find the size of each state’s populatio

(B) to keep track of aliens

(C) to count senior citizens

(D) to find the number of children in each family

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The census tells us who we are and where we are going as a nation, and helps our communities determine where to build everything from schools to supermarkets, and from homes to hospitals. It helps the government decide how to distribute funds and assistance to states and localities.

Concept note-2: -The U.S. census counts every resident in the United States. It is mandated by Article I, Section 2 of the Constitution and takes place every 10 years. The Economic Census is the official five-year measure of American businesses providing comprehensive statistics at the national, state, and local levels.

Concept note-3: -The process of a government counting the citizens of its country is called a census.

Concept note-4: -The process of a government counting the citizens of its country is called a census. In other words, “the official enumeration of citizens done periodically by the government is a census. The word ‘census’ is derived from the Latin word ‘centre’ meaning ‘to assess or to rate. ‘ “
