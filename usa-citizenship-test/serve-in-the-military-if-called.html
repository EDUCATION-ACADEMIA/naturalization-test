[SOURCES]
serve in the military if called

(A) ** duty

(B) responsibility

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -conscripts; conscripted; conscripting If you’re ever drafted into the army, then you could be called a conscript, someone who is forced to join the military.

Concept note-2: -Enlisted personnel typically do the following: Participate in, or support, military operations, such as combat or training operations, or humanitarian or disaster relief. Operate, maintain, and repair equipment. Perform technical and support activities.

Concept note-3: -Being an active duty sailor means that the US Navy is your job-your day job, your night job, your ‘real’ job. The Navy is the overseer of your household and livelihood. It dictates where you will go when you will go there, and how long you will stay there. This place the Navy sends you is called a duty station.
