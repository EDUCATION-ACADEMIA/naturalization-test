A ____ decision would include saving money.

(A) Personal

(B) Duty

(C) ** Financial

(D) Summoned

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -But to economists, saving means only one thing-consuming less out of a given amount of resources in the present in order to consume more in the future. Saving, therefore, is the decision to defer consumption and to store this deferred consumption in some form of asset.

Concept note-2: -Saving provides a financial “backstop” for life’s uncertainties and increases feelings of security and peace of mind. Once an adequate emergency fund is established, savings can also provide the “seed money” for higher-yielding investments such as stocks, bonds, and mutual funds.
