The first form of national government for the independent states was created by the ____ ? ____

(A) Declarationof Independence

(B) ** Articlesof Confederation

(C) MayflowerCompact

(D) VirginiaDeclaration of Rights

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Articles of Confederation were adopted by the Continental Congress on November 15, 1777. This document served as the United Statesâ€™ first constitution. It was in force from March 1, 1781, until 1789 when the present-day Constitution went into effect.

Concept note-2: -The Articles created a national government centered on the legislative branch, which was comprised of a single house. There was no separate executive branch or judicial branch. The delegates in Congress voted by state-with each state receiving one vote, regardless of its population.

Concept note-3: -The Articles created a loose confederation of sovereign states and a weak central government, leaving most of the power with the state governments. The need for a stronger Federal government soon became apparent and eventually led to the Constitutional Convention in 1787.
