What is one promise we make when we become US citizens?

(A) vote

(B) ** obey the laws of the country

(C) serve on a jury

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Key principle: I promise that I will protect the Constitution and all laws from all enemies, from other countries, or from inside the United States. SECTION 3 …that I will bear true faith and allegiance to the same; Key principle: I promise that my loyalty is to the United States only.

Concept note-2: -obey the laws of the United States. serve in the U.S. military (if needed) serve (do important work for) the nation (if needed) be loyal to the United States.

Concept note-3: -An oath of citizenship is designed to be a statement of patriotism and loyalty to the new country.

Concept note-4: -Congratulations on your U.S.A. citizenship! You have worked hard and dedicated your life to America. Wishing you peace, freedom, and a bright future. Congratulations on achieving citizenship in the United States of America.

Concept note-5: -The Pledge of Allegiance is a promise to be loyal to the United States. “I pledge allegiance to the flag of the United States of America.
