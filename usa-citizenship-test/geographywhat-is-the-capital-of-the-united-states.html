Geography:What is the capital of the United States?

(A) New York, New York

(B) ** Washington, D.C.

(C) Boston, Massachusetts

(D) Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Washington D.C. has no capital. It is a federal district and is the capital of the United States of America.

Concept note-2: -The Constitution dictates that the federal district be under the jurisdiction of the US Congress. Washington, DC operates as a state while also performing functions of a city and a county. We are treated as a state in more than 500 federal laws.

Concept note-3: -The city is located in the central eastern part of the US on the Potomac River, bordering the U.S. state of Virginia in the west and the U.S. state of Maryland in the east.
