Which of the following is NOT a role of political parties?

(A) Identify important issues

(B) Raising funds

(C) ** Setting Presidential agendas

(D) Selecting Candidates

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thus, Controlling the judiciary is NOT a function of political parties.

Concept note-2: -Functions of political parties: i Parties contest elections. ii They put forward policies and programmes. iii Parties play a decisive role in making laws. iv Parties form and run a government.

Concept note-3: -The primary function of a political party is to contest election, through which they try to form government.

Concept note-4: -A political party basically, is a group of people. These people come together to contest elections in order to hold power in the government. It is a way to mobilize voters to support common sets of interests, concerns, and goals. The primary role of the political party is to fix the political agenda and policies.
