pay taxes

(A) ** duty

(B) responsibility

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As per the Direct taxation laws; income and other kinds of taxes are a legal obligation for an Indian citizen as he uses public services, receives social and political security from the state etc.

Concept note-2: -Duty to pay taxes shall not be a fundamental duty. Punishment to pay for the violation of fundamental duties.

Concept note-3: -The term “duty” refers to a form of taxation levied on certain goods, services, or other transactions. People and corporations may be required to pay levies on imports and exports by governments in the form of customs duties and other taxes.

Concept note-4: -Taxes are used by the government for carrying out various welfare schemes including employment programmes. There are Lakhs of employees in various departments and the administrative cost has to be borne by the Government.

Concept note-5: -Any Indian person under the age of 60 who earns more than Rs 2.5 lakh is required to pay income tax.
