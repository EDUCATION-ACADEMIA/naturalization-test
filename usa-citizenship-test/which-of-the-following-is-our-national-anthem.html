Which of the following is our national anthem?

(A) My Country ‘tis of Thee

(B) America, the Beautiful

(C) ** The Star-Spangled Banner

(D) The Pledge of Allegiance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“Star-Spangled Banner” facts and figures: In 1916, President Woodrow Wilson signed an Executive Order to make it our national anthem. In 1931, President Herbert Hoover signed a Public Law that made it the official national anthem.

Concept note-2: -The National Anthem of India Jana-gana-mana, composed originally in Bengali by Rabindranath Tagore, was adopted in its Hindi version by the Constituent Assembly as the National Anthem of India on 24 January 1950.

Concept note-3: -The official national anthem of the United States of American is called “The Star-Spangled Banner". The anthem is taken from a poem written in 1814 by American poet Francis Scott Key, after he witnessed the siege of Fort McHenry by the British during the War of 1812.

Concept note-4: -Definition of ‘Star-Spangled Banner’ 1. the national anthem of the United States of America. 2. another term for the Stars and Stripes.
