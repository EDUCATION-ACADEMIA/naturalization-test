Which of the following is not a power of the legislative branch?

(A) The power to override a veto

(B) The power to reject treaties

(C) ** The power to appoint judges

(D) The power to impeach Supreme Court justices

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Their powers may include passing laws, establishing the government’s budget, confirming executive appointments, ratifying treaties, investigating the executive branch, impeaching and removing from office members of the executive and judiciary, and redressing constituents’ grievances.

Concept note-2: -The Constitution grants Congress the sole authority to enact legislation and declare war, the right to confirm or reject many Presidential appointments, and substantial investigative powers.

Concept note-3: -This authority is given by the constitution as a part of supreme legislation. So, in India, the authority is possessed by the parliament.

Concept note-4: -The judiciary is that branch of the government that interprets the law, settles disputes and administers justice to all citizens. The judiciary is considered the watchdog of democracy, and also the guardian of the Constitution.
