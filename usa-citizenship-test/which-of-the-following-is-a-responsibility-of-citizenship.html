Which of the following is a Responsibility of Citizenship?

(A) ** Petitioning the government

(B) Jury Duty

(C) Helping elderly people

(D) Paying Taxes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Citizens have a responsibility to participate in the political process by registering and voting in elections. Serving on a jury is another responsibility of citizenship.

Concept note-2: -T. H. Marshall (1950) defined citizenship as ‘full membership of a community’. According to him, citizenship is constituted by three elements: civil, political and social (which are resumed in the following scheme).

Concept note-3: -Indian Constitution provides for single citizenship which means that there is no concept of state citizenship. Also, one cannot have of India and any other country simultaneously.
