Relying on yourself and using your own resources (money).

(A) Patriotism

(B) Trustworthiness

(C) ** Self-reliance

(D) Respect for the Law

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Self-reliance is the ability to do things and make decisions by yourself, without needing other people to help you.

Concept note-2: -Of no surprise is his favoring the bucolic life. Emerson now focuses on four social arenas in which self-reliant individuals are needed: religion, which fears creativity; culture, which devalues individualism; the arts, which teach us only to imitate; and society, which falsely values so-called progress.

Concept note-3: -Self-reliance is related to control over decision making, whereas self-suf ciency refers to ful lling an individual’s or group’s physical needs, and thus is linked to resource use. Territoriality can also be interpreted in terms of decision making and resource use.
