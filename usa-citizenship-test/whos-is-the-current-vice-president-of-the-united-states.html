Whos is the current Vice President of the United States?

(A) ** Kamala Harris

(B) Mike Pence

(C) Joe Biden

(D) George W. Bush

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kamala Devi Harris (/ˈkɑːmələ ˈdeɪvi/ ( listen) KAH-mə-lə DAY-vee; born October 20, 1964) is an American politician and attorney who is the 49th and current vice president of the United States.

Concept note-2: -Kamala D. Harris is the Vice President of the United States of America. She was elected Vice President after a lifetime of public service, having been elected District Attorney of San Francisco, California Attorney General, and United States Senator.

Concept note-3: -The youngest living vice president is the incumbent, Kamala Harris, born on October 20, 1964 (age 58 years, 124 days). The shortest-lived vice president was Daniel D. Tompkins, who died at the age of 50 years, 355 days, only 99 days after leaving office.

Concept note-4: -Under the Constitution, the vice president serves as the president of the Senate and presides over the Senate’s daily proceedings.
