Why does the flag have 13 stripes?

(A) Because each state represents a colony

(B) Because each star represents a state

(C) ** Because each stripe represents a colony

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -How many stars and stripes are on the flag? There are 50 stars representing the 50 states and there are 13 stripes representing the 13 original colonies.

Concept note-2: -The 50 stars on the flag represent the 50 states within the United States and the 13 stripes represent the 13 British Colonies that rebelled against the British Monarchy and became the first states in the Union.
