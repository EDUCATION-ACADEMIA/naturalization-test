Which of the following person is a US Citizen

(A) ** Sally, she uses a US passport to travel to Mexico

(B) Jay, he was born in Japan on a Chinese military base

(C) Mike, he was born to immigrant parents in Canada

(D) Tiffany, she was born in Taiwan and immigrated to the US at the age of 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to this infographic on the Mexican City Airport website, you’ll need to submit a valid passport and a boarding pass to travel within Mexico via air. However, there have been reported cases where US citizens could board a domestic flight (within Mexico) with their passport card and enhanced driver’s license.

Concept note-2: -Do US Citizens Need A Visa To Go To Mexico? No, US citizens do not need a Mexica Visa. US citizens enjoy visa-free travel to Mexico for up to 180 days.

Concept note-3: -A valid passport book is required to enter Mexico by air, and those attempting to enter at an airport with a U.S. passport card only may be denied admission.

Concept note-4: -Indians with valid and multiple entries from the USA, Canada, Japan, United Kingdom, Schengen Visa, or permanent residence will not need a Mexican visa.
