Which is not a promise you make when you become a US citizen?

(A) Obey the laws of the US

(B) Be loyal to the US

(C) ** Never leave the USA again

(D) Give up loyalty to other countries

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Support and defend the U.S. Constitution and the laws of the United States against its enemies. Give up allegiance to any other nation or sovereign, and renounce hereditary or noble titles, if any. Provide military or civilian service when called upon by the government to do so.

Concept note-2: -Key principle: I promise that I will protect the Constitution and all laws from all enemies, from other countries, or from inside the United States. SECTION 3 …that I will bear true faith and allegiance to the same; Key principle: I promise that my loyalty is to the United States only.

Concept note-3: -obey the laws of the United States. serve in the U.S. military (if needed) serve (do important work for) the nation (if needed) be loyal to the United States.
