In order for a United States citizen to receive a fair trial, other citizens must ____

(A) evaluate judges

(B) vote in elections

(C) pay poll taxes

(D) ** serve on juries

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Sixth Amendment guarantees the rights of criminal defendants, including the right to a public trial without unnecessary delay, the right to a lawyer, the right to an impartial jury, and the right to know who your accusers are and the nature of the charges and evidence against you.

Concept note-2: -: a trial that is conducted fairly, justly, and with procedural regularity by an impartial judge and in which the defendant is afforded his or her rights under the U.S. Constitution or the appropriate state constitution or other law.

Concept note-3: -It is the duty of the state to ensure that every citizen of the country should have the free and fair investigation and trial.

Concept note-4: -The right to an impartial jury; The right to due process of law; The right to confront/call witnesses; and. The right to legal counsel.
