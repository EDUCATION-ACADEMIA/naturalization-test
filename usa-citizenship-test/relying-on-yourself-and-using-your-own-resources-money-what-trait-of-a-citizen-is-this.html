Relying on yourself and using your own resources (money). What trait of a citizen is this?

(A) Trustworthiness

(B) Patriotism

(C) Participation in elections as an informed voter

(D) ** Self-Reliance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The idea that one can rely on his or her own judgment, choices, and be free from these societal influences is to be self-reliant.

Concept note-2: -Self-reliance is the ability to do things and make decisions by yourself, without needing other people to help you. People learned self-reliance because they had to. The Prime Minister called for more economic self-reliance.

Concept note-3: -More extreme examples of self-reliance include people who live “off the grid”, using solar panels, growing their own food, and taking other steps to minimize their dependence on the infrastructure the rest of us take for granted.

Concept note-4: -autonomous, self-sufficient, self-supporting, self-sustaining, on one’s own, self-contained, self-governing, unaided, unallied.

Concept note-5: -: reliance on one’s own efforts and abilities.
