Under the American system of government citizens have

(A) Few rights and responsibilities

(B) ** Many rights and responsibilities

(C) no rights and responsibilities

(D) Unlimited powers and can do what the want

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Respect and obey federal, state, and local laws. Respect the rights, beliefs, and opinions of others. Participate in your local community. Pay income and other taxes honestly, and on time, to federal, state, and local authorities.

Concept note-2: -Such rights include not only the right to vote in an election, but also the rights to join a political party; run for office; and participate freely in political rallies, events, or protests.

Concept note-3: -Some of these rights are freedom of expression, of religion, of speech, and the right to bear arms. The Constitution and the Bill of Rights give many of these rights to all people living in the United States. All people living in the United States must obey laws, including local, state, and federal ones.
