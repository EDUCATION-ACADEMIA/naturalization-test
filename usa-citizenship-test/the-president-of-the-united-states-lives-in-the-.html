The president of the United States lives in the ____

(A) ** White House

(B) Atlanta, Georgia

(C) The Capitol

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The White House is where the President and First Family of the United States live and work-but it’s also the People’s House, where we hope all Americans feel a sense of inclusion and belonging.

Concept note-2: -By tradition, the President and the First Family live in the White House in Washington, D.C., also the location of the President’s Oval Office and the offices of his or her senior staff.

Concept note-3: -The Executive Residence is the central building of the White House complex located between the East Wing and West Wing.

Concept note-4: -While President George Washington selected the site and approved the design, he never actually lived in the White House-he ended his service as chief executive in 1797 and died in 1799, one year before the seat of the federal government moved from Philadelphia to the city named in his honor.
