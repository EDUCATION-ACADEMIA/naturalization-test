In Williamsburg, Virginia, a group held a meeting to discuss supporting a candidate who was running for mayor. Which constitutional right allowed this meeting to take place?

(A) The right to a trial by jury

(B) ** Freedom of assembly

(C) Freedom of the press

(D) The right to vote

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Civic participation encompasses a wide range of formal and informal activities, such as voting, volunteering, participating in group activities, and community gardening.

Concept note-2: -Examples of civic duties include serving on a jury, voting in elections, or participating in community service projects.

Concept note-3: -Examples of civic responsibility include voting in elections, signing up for the military, volunteering in the community, participating in government politics, and holding public office.
