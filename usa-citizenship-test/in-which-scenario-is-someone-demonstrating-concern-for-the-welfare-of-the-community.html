In which scenario is someone demonstrating concern for the welfare of the community?

(A) Rebecca visits her grandmother in a local nursing home every Sunday evening.

(B) Vanessa saves her allowance so she can purchase video games from a local store

(C) Derick mows his grass and maintains the plants in his front yard.

(D) ** Tyson collects canned foods from local businesses to take to the local food pantry.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Civic participation encompasses a wide range of formal and informal activities, such as voting, volunteering, participating in group activities, and community gardening.

Concept note-2: -Examples of civic responsibility include voting in elections, signing up for the military, volunteering in the community, participating in government politics, and holding public office.

Concept note-3: -Why is community service important in a democratic nation? It addresses local needs and serves the public good.
