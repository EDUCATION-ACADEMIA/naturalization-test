What is a citizen?

(A) A member of Congress

(B) A member of a political party

(C) ** An official member of a country

(D) A member of a soccer team

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Citizenship is the expression of a public identity. It is the recognition of an official position by a government and the ability to enjoy the rights and privileges following from that position by an individual. Citizenship is also the subjective belonging to the public identity of a country.

Concept note-2: -A citizen is a person who, by place of birth, nationality of one or both parents, or naturalization is granted full rights and responsibilities as a member of a nation or political community.

Concept note-3: -A citizen is a participatory member of a political community. Citizenship is gained by meeting the legal requirements of a national, state, or local government. A nation grants certain rights and privileges to its citizens. In return, citizens are expected to obey their countryâ€™s laws and defend it against its enemies.

Concept note-4: -A citizen is born in the country and entitled by law to enjoy certain rights and perform certain duties mentioned in the constitution of a country.

Concept note-5: -Citizen is a person of a country who is entitled to enjoy all the legal rights and privileges granted by a state and is obligated to obey its laws and to fulfil his duties.
