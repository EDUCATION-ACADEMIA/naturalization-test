EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What percentage of American couples are childless? A staggering 57% of American households are child-free. Among the baby boomer generation, the rate of people who are aging solo is nearly 20%.

Concept note-2: -The share of the U.S.’s 130 million households headed by married parents with children under age 18 fell to 17.8% in 2021 from 18.6% last year, according to the Census Bureau. That’s down from more than 40% in 1970.

Concept note-3: -Just the two of us: 1 in 4 couples opt to never have kids-and are just as happy.
