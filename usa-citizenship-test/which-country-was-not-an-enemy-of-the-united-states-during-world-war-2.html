Which country was not an enemy of the United States during World War 2?

(A) ** Russia

(B) Italy

(C) Japan

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Only 14 countries remained officially neutral throughout the entire war. They included Sweden, Switzerland, Spain, Portugal, Ireland, Turkey, Yemen, Saudi Arabia and Afghanistan as well as the microstates of Andorra, Monaco, Liechtenstein, San Marino and Vatican City.

Concept note-2: -On December 8, President Roosevelt asked Congress to declare war against Japan. The declaration passed with just one dissenting vote. Three days later, Germany and Italy, allied with Japan, declared war on the United States.

Concept note-3: -The principal belligerents were the Axis powers-Germany, Italy, and Japan-and the Allies-France, Great Britain, the United States, the Soviet Union, and, to a lesser extent, China.

Concept note-4: -Following the German invasion, the USSR allied itself with the UK and the US against Nazi Germany and Italy, although it maintained peace with Japan until August 1945.
