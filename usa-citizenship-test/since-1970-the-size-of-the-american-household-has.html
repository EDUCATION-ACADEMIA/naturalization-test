Since 1970, the size of the American household has

(A) More than tripled

(B) More than doubled

(C) Stayed the same

(D) ** Decreased

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In concordance with the rise of single-person households, the percentage of family households with own children living in the household has declined since 1970 from 56 percent to 40 percent in 2020.

Concept note-2: -Of the 51.9 million families in the United States, 8.9 percent received incomes below $3, 000 in 1970.

Concept note-3: -Since the number of households Increased faster than the population, the average household size declined from 3.20 persons per household in 1970 to 2.74 In 1980. In-uses the “head of household” designation.

Concept note-4: -The number of households in the United States in 1960 was 53.0 million. The average size of household was 3.29 persons.
