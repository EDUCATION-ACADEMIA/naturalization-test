These are two primary ways to become a US citizen.

(A) ** Native Born

(B) Traveling

(C) Cousins

(D) Naturalization

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are two ways to become a United States (U.S.) citizen – by birth or through naturalization.

Concept note-2: -There are two general ways to obtain citizenship through U.S. citizen parents: at birth, and after birth but before the age of 18. Congress has enacted laws that determine how citizenship is conveyed by a U.S. citizen parent (or parents) to children born outside of the United States.

Concept note-3: -There are two kinds of citizenships in India. One is ‘natural’-by birth and the other is ‘acquired’ citizenship. If you’re born in India and your parents are Indian citizens, then you’re an Indian citizen. The other is ‘acquired’ wherein people can acquire Indian citizenship on the basis of certain rules.
