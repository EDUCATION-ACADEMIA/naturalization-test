Sasha is an 8th grade student. Once a week she volunteers to tutor 5th graders at a local elementary school. What is Sasha demonstrating?

(A) ** Community service

(B) Community rights

(C) Civic rights

(D) Civic duties

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“Community engagement is important as it takes action to influence stakeholders with government, political or funding power to implement public projects and policies that primarily benefit individual communities and drive social change.”

Concept note-2: -Examples of civic duties include serving on a jury, voting in elections, or participating in community service projects.

Concept note-3: -Citizens vote for leaders to represent them and their ideas, and the leaders support the citizens’ interests. There are two special rights only for U.S. citizens: voting in federal elections and running for federal office. Many naturalized citizens have been elected as U.S. Senators and U.S. Representatives.
