Responsibility, accountability and self reliance are:

(A) all examples of civic responsibilities

(B) ** all traits of good citizens

(C) all examples of civic duties

(D) all functions of political parties

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Identify characteristics of good citizenship including truthfulness, justice, equality, respect for oneself and others, responsibility in daily life, and participation in government by educating oneself about the issues, respectfully holding public officials to their word, and voting.

Concept note-2: -Integrity. Respect. Responsibility. Understanding, Tolerance and Inclusion.

Concept note-3: -There are various qualities to being a good citizen, such as abiding by the law, staying educated and informed, be involved in the process of democracy, act in the best interest of others, and believing that everyone is equal.
