EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nancy Patricia Pelosi (/pəˈloʊsi/; née D’Alesandro; born March 26, 1940) is an American politician who served as the 52nd speaker of the United States House of Representatives from 2007 to 2011 and again from 2019 to 2023.

Concept note-2: -Altogether, 55 individuals, from 23 states, have served as speaker of the House. The office is currently held by Kevin McCarthy following the outcome of the 2023 speaker election conducted at the start of the 118th Congress.

Concept note-3: -Today, Speaker of the House and Representative of California’s 20th Congressional District, Kevin McCarthy, attended the World Ag Expo in Tulare, California.

Concept note-4: -The 128th U.S. speaker election since the office was created in 1789, it began on January 3, 2023, and concluded in the early morning hours of January 7 when Kevin McCarthy of California, leader of the House Republican Conference, won a majority of votes cast on the fifteenth ballot.

Concept note-5: -James K. Polk of Tennessee was the first (and only) Speaker to serve as President.
