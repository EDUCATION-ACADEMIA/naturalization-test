EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Citizenship is the status of a person recognized under the law as being a legal member of a sovereign state or belonging to a nation. It gives him/her some rights and privileges along with duties and obligations towards the nation.

Concept note-2: -A citizen is a person who, by place of birth, nationality of one or both parents, or naturalization is granted full rights and responsibilities as a member of a nation or political community. [Last updated in January of 2022 by the Wex Definitions Team] CIVICS.

Concept note-3: -A citizen is a participatory member of a political community. Citizenship is gained by meeting the legal requirements of a national, state, or local government. A nation grants certain rights and privileges to its citizens. In return, citizens are expected to obey their country’s laws and defend it against its enemies.

Concept note-4: -There are two kinds of citizenships in India. One is ‘natural’-by birth and the other is ‘acquired’ citizenship.
