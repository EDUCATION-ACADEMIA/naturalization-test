Which of the answers below is required for all 18 year old male citizens?

(A) Voting in federal elections

(B) ** Registering with the U.S. Selective Service

(C) Voting in local elections

(D) EnlistingintheArmy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Almost all men ages 18-25 who are U.S. citizens or are immigrants living in the U.S. are required to register with Selective Service.

Concept note-2: -What is Selective Service registration? Registration is a way our government keeps a list of names of men from which to draw in case of a national emergency requiring rapid expansion of our Armed Forces. By registering all young men, Selective Service ensures that a future draft will be fair and equitable.

Concept note-3: -Registration with the Selective Services is the process by which the U.S. government collects names and addresses of men ages 18 through 25 to use in case a national emergency requires rapid expansion of the armed forces. Registration is mandatory for male U.S. citizens ages 18 through 25.

Concept note-4: -May 1917-Congress passes the Selective Service Act, establishing local, district, state, and territorial civilian boards to register men between the ages of 21 and 30 for service in World War One.
