All of the following are legal means of becoming a citizen EXCEPT:

(A) Born in USA

(B) Born to Citizen parents

(C) Naturalized

(D) ** Having a relative who is American

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are two ways to become a United States (U.S.) citizen – by birth or through naturalization.

Concept note-2: -You need to be at least 18 years old, be a permanent resident with a green card for a least 5 years, 3 years if you are married to a U.S citizen. You must maintain continuous residence in the U.S., at least 3 months in California, and not have any trips outside the U.S. for 6 months or longer.

Concept note-3: -Complete answer: Option B) Acquiring property – may be a correct answer because it is not a condition for becoming the citizen of India. Indian citizenship act was passed in 1955.
