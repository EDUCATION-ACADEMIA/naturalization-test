Civics:What is the economic system in the United States?

(A) ** Capitalist

(B) Socialist

(C) Communist

(D) Mercantilist

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Capitalism is the economic system in the United States. It is a market economy. Capitalism means that people, not the government, own most businesses. In the U.S., businesses decide what to sell.

Concept note-2: -There are many different types of economic systems used throughout the world. Some examples are socialism, communism, and capitalism. The United States has a capitalistic system.

Concept note-3: -Wall Street and the stock market embody capitalism. Large, publicly-traded companies sell stock to raise capital, which is bought-and-sold by investors via a system in which prices are directly impacted by supply and demand. Everyone can participate, from individual investors to major financial investment firms.
