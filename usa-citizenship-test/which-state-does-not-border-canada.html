Which state does not border Canada?

(A) Pennsylvania

(B) Maine

(C) ** Oregon

(D) Washington

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thirteen states lie on the U.S.–Canada border . The U.S. states of Indiana, Illinois, and Wisconsin do not share a direct geographic border with Canada.

Concept note-2: -Distance between Oregon and Canadian Border, Welcome Sign is 602.21 km. This distance is equal to 374.2 miles, and 324.95 nautical miles.

Concept note-3: -Alaska and Hawaii are the only two states that do not share a border with any other part of the U.S. Alaska is surrounded by the Arctic and Pacific oceans on its North, South, and West sides.

Concept note-4: -Maine is the easternmost US state, situated in the northeastern part of the country, bordering Canada. It is also the northernmost of the six New England states. Maine borders the Canadian provinces of Québec to the northwest and New Brunswick to the north and northeast.
