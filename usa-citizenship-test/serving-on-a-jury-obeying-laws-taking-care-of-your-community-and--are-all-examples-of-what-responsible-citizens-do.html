Serving on a jury, obeying laws, taking care of your community, and ____ are all examples of what responsible citizens do.

(A) ** Voting

(B) Giving money to the sheriff’s fund

(C) Eating your veggies

(D) Learning to dance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Treat others with consideration and regard, respect another person’s point of view. Responsibility. Be accountable for one’s own actions, resolve differences in constructive, non-violent and peaceful ways, contribute to society and to civic life, take care of the environment.

Concept note-2: -Civic participation encompasses a wide range of formal and informal activities, such as voting, volunteering, participating in group activities, and community gardening.

Concept note-3: -Civic responsibility means active participation in the public life of a community in an informed, committed, and constructive manner, with a focus on the common good.
