The ____ Plan proposed representation based on population of the state?

(A) ** Virginia

(B) New Jersey

(C) Connecticut

(D) Three-fifths

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The plan called for a legislature divided into two bodies (the Senate and the House of Representatives) with proportional representation. That is, each state’s representation in Congress would be based on its population.

Concept note-2: -The Virginia, or large state, plan provided for a bicameral legislature with representation of each state based on its population or wealth; the New Jersey, or small state, plan proposed equal representation for each state in Congress.

Concept note-3: -William Paterson’s New Jersey Plan proposed a unicameral (one-house) legislature with equal votes of states and an executive elected by a national legislature. This plan maintained the form of government under the Articles of Confederation while adding powers to raise revenue and regulate commerce and foreign affairs.

Concept note-4: -The vote on the Virginia Plan was to move from equal representation to a population-based system.
