How many members are there in the House of Representatives for the State of Oregon?

(A) 15

(B) 40

(C) ** 60

(D) 435

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Oregon House of Representatives consists of 60 elected members.

Concept note-2: -House District 60 (Baker, Grant, Harney, Lake and Malheur Counties, and parts of Deschutes County)

Concept note-3: -There are currently 435 voting representatives. Five delegates and one resident commissioner serve as non-voting members of the House, although they can vote in committee. Representatives must be 25 years old and must have been U.S. citizens for at least 7 years.

Concept note-4: -Suzanne Bonamici-First District Elected to the U.S. House of Representatives, 2012; reelected 2014, 2016, 2018, 2020 and 2022.

Concept note-5: -The Oregon State Senate consists of 30 elected members.
