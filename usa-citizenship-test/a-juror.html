(A) makes laws

(D) ** is part of a jury

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The jury consists of 12 members of the public who sit in a box to one side of the judge. One of the jurors is selected as a foreman of the jury by the members of the jury before the case starts. He or she acts as an informal chairperson and spokesperson for the jury.

Concept note-2: -ˈjər-: a member of a jury. : a person summoned to serve on a jury. : a person who takes an oath (as of allegiance)

Concept note-3: -A Judge is an individual who is tasked with presiding over a case proceeding while a jury is a group of people who have sworn in to give a verdict to a case as is given to them by the court.

Concept note-4: -But fundamental difference between Jury System and judge system is : Jury System depends on a few randomly chosen citizens from ENTIRE population, and DIFFERENT Jurors are used for different cases; WHILE judge system uses same appointed individuals for almost all cases that would come.
