Which statement best expresses the principle of “consent of the governed”?

(A) The Congress shall assemble at least once in every year.

(B) All Bills for raising Revenue shall originate in the House of Representatives.

(C) ** We the People of the United States ____ establish this Constitution

(D) No Person except a natural born Citizen ____ shall be eligible to the Office of President

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The statement that best expresses the purpose of the opening words of the us constitution “we the people” would be “a. political sovereignty stems from the consent of the people, ” since this was the main grievance regarding the British that the colonists were ultimately fighting against.

Concept note-2: -Which statement best describes the principle of Consent of the Governed? Citizens give authority to the government in exchange for protection of their rights.

Concept note-3: -consent of the governed (uncountable) (government, political science, philosophy) A political theory in which a government or set of governments could only be deemed legitimate if it is supported by the people under which it exercises its political influence.

Concept note-4: -The concept of public opinion arose as a consequence of the rise of democracy, premised on the notion that government should rule with the consent of the governed.

Concept note-5: -Social contract theory, nearly as old as philosophy itself, is the view that persons’ moral and/or political obligations are dependent upon a contract or agreement among them to form the society in which they live.
