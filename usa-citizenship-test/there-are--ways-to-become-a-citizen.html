There are ____ ways to become a citizen

(A) ** two

(B) three

(C) 100

(D) 435

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are two ways to become a United States (U.S.) citizen – by birth or through naturalization.

Concept note-2: -The Constitution of India does not allow holding Indian citizenship and citizenship of a foreign country simultaneously. Based on the recommendation of the High Level committee on Indian Diaspora, the Government of India decided to grant Overseas Citizenship of India (OCI) commonly known as “dual citizenship".

Concept note-3: -The Vice President is the second citizen of India.
