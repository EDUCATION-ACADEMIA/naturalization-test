What Ocean is on the West?

(A) Gulf of Mexico

(B) Atlantic Ocean

(C) ** Pacific Ocean

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The IAPB Western Pacific Region covers 28 diverse countries in the Pacific, Oceania and parts of Asia. The region stretches over a vast area, from China and Mongolia in the north, to New Zealand in the south.

Concept note-2: -The Pacific Ocean is on the West Coast of the United States. The Pacific Ocean is the largest ocean in the world. It reaches from the West Coast of the United States to Asia and Australia. The states of Alaska, Washington, Oregon, California, and Hawaii border the Pacific Ocean.

Concept note-3: -The Pacific Ocean (from the Latin name Mare Pacificum, “peaceful sea", bestowed upon it by the Portuguese explorer Ferdinand Magellan) is the world’s largest body of water.
