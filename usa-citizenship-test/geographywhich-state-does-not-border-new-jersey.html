Geography:Which state DOES NOT border New Jersey?

(A) Pennsylvania

(B) New York

(C) Delaware

(D) ** Connecticut

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -GEOGRAPHY AND LANDFORMS This New England state is bordered by Massachusetts in the north, Rhode Island in the east, a body of water called the Long Island Sound in the south, and New York in the west. Connecticut can be divided into three geographic regions.

Concept note-2: -GEOGRAPHY AND LANDFORMS New Jersey is bordered by Pennsylvania and New York in the north, Pennsylvania in the west, Delaware and Delaware Bay in the south, the Atlantic Ocean in west, and Long Island (a part of New York) in the east.

Concept note-3: -Distance from Connecticut to New Jersey is 204 kilometers. This air travel distance is equal to 127 miles. The air travel (bird fly) shortest distance between Connecticut and New Jersey is 204 km= 127 miles.
