What is the form of government where citizens elect a small group to govern for them?

(A) Communism

(B) Monarchy

(C) ** Representative Government

(D) Rule of Law

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Oligarchy is a form of government in which a small group of people hold most or all political power.

Concept note-2: -Representative democracy is a kind of democracy in which people elect their leaders. It is also known as indirect democracy or representative government. Representative democracy is important since it helps the people to know their representatives before electing them.

Concept note-3: -Democratic Governments You now know that the main feature of a democracy is that the people have the power to elect their leaders. So in a sense a democracy is rule by the people.

Concept note-4: -Representative democracy places power in the hands of representatives who are elected by the people.
