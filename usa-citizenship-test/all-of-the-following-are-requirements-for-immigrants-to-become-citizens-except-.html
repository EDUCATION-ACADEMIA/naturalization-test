All of the following are requirements for immigrants to become citizens EXCEPT ____

(A) Resident of the U.S. for 5 years

(B) ** Provide letters of recommendation

(C) Able to read, write, and speak English

(D) Pass a citizenship test and pledge loyalty to the U.S.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To become a U.S. citizen, you must: Have had a Permanent Resident (Green) Card for at least five years, or for at least three years if you’re filing as the spouse of a U.S. citizen.

Concept note-2: -Demonstrate good moral character; Demonstrate a knowledge and understanding of U.S. history and government; Demonstrate a loyalty to the principles of the U.S. Constitution; and. Be willing to take the Oath of Allegiance.

Concept note-3: -Indian passport or birth certificate. A copy of marriage certificate issued by the Registrar of Marriage. before the offices specified in the Citizenship Rules, 1955 i.e. Collector/ DM/ DC. 3 Form IV, Section 5 (1) (d) • A copy of valid Foreign Passport of the child or parent or which has/ her name is entered.

Concept note-4: -Complete answer: Option B) Acquiring property – may be a correct answer because it is not a condition for becoming the citizen of India.
